# LinkedIn About — draft

I am a geoinformatics researcher and geospatial developer working at the intersection of geocomputing, GeoAI, Earth Observation and GIS software.

My work focuses on transforming spatial data into reproducible research, open-source tools and usable geospatial systems. I have contributed to initiatives such as rgee and CloudSEN12, and I currently work on applied geotechnologies involving remote sensing, AI, WebGIS, photogrammetry and 3D reconstruction.

I combine a background in Geographic Engineering, a Master's degree in Computer Science and more than 10 years of experience across public institutions, academia and international research projects. I am also developing a stronger innovation and product-oriented profile, connecting research methods with project management, product ownership, prototyping and technology transfer.

My long-term goal is to contribute to the development of a strong research group in Peru focused on geocomputing, GeoAI, Earth Observation and open geospatial technologies.

I am interested in collaborations involving applied research, GeoAI, spatial data infrastructure, WebGIS, environmental monitoring, 3D geospatial reconstruction and geospatial education.
