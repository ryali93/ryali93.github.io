## GeoAI & Earth Observation

Semantic segmentation, change detection, cloud and shadow detection, data harmonization, super-resolution and reproducible deep learning workflows for satellite imagery.

## Geocomputing & Spatial Data Engineering

Spatial data cubes, STAC, Google Earth Engine, Sentinel Hub, Planetary Computer, PostGIS, cloud-native geospatial pipelines and scalable Python/R workflows.

## GIS Software & WebGIS

Open-source geospatial architectures, WebGIS applications, APIs, GeoServer, dashboards, interoperability and spatial decision-support systems.

## Environmental & Risk Monitoring

Earth Observation for forests, protected areas, fires, soil moisture, vegetation dynamics, land cover change, hazards and climate-related risks.

## 3D Geospatial Reconstruction

Structure from Motion, NeRF, Gaussian Splatting, photogrammetry and point clouds for heritage, forensic analysis, infrastructure and spatial documentation.

## Teaching & Research Leadership

Geospatial programming, reproducible science, open-source software and the long-term development of a research group in Peru focused on geocomputing and GeoAI.
