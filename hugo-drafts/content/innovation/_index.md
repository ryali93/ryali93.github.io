My innovation path is based on a simple principle: geospatial research should become usable knowledge, software and institutional capacity.

## How I connect research and product thinking

1. **Research problem** — define a scientific or operational challenge involving spatial data.
2. **Computational method** — design models, pipelines and validation strategies.
3. **Geospatial product** — transform methods into software, datasets, APIs, dashboards or WebGIS platforms.
4. **Adoption and training** — document, teach, maintain and transfer the solution to users and institutions.

This direction strengthens my academic profile while adding project management, product ownership and innovation capabilities to my research practice.
