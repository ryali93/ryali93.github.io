CloudSEN12 is a global dataset for cloud and shadow semantic understanding in Sentinel-2 imagery. It is one of the strongest pieces in my academic narrative because it connects Earth Observation, data curation, semantic segmentation and open science.

## Suggested case-study structure

- **Problem:** reliable cloud and shadow masks are essential for downstream satellite image analysis.
- **Role:** contributor to dataset/research workflow.
- **Methods:** Sentinel-2, semantic segmentation, annotation analysis, reproducible EO workflows.
- **Impact:** open dataset and peer-reviewed publication.
- **Links:** paper, dataset, code, citation.
