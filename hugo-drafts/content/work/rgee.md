rgee is an R package that connects R users with Google Earth Engine. It should be positioned as a flagship open-source contribution in geocomputation and reproducible Earth Observation.

## Suggested case-study structure

- **Problem:** bridge the R geospatial ecosystem with cloud-scale Earth Observation.
- **Role:** contributor to software and publication.
- **Methods:** R, Python bridge, Google Earth Engine, reproducible workflows.
- **Impact:** open-source software and JOSS publication.
- **Links:** GitHub, paper, documentation, citation.
