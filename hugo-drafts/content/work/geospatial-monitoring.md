This project family groups my work on WebGIS, Earth Observation and AI-based monitoring systems. It includes protected areas monitoring, infrastructure monitoring, forest monitoring and institutional dashboards.

## Suggested case-study structure

- **Problem:** institutions need reproducible, transparent and scalable monitoring systems.
- **Role:** GIS developer, remote sensing specialist and geoinformatics researcher.
- **Stack:** Python, R, Google Earth Engine, PostGIS, GeoServer, FastAPI, NodeJS, React/Angular.
- **Outputs:** dashboards, APIs, geospatial services, scripts and decision-support tools.
- **Impact:** conservation, infrastructure and risk monitoring.
