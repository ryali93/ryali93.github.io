3D reconstruction is a strong applied research line within my broader geocomputing profile. It connects photogrammetry, computer vision, point clouds and new neural representations.

## Suggested case-study structure

- **Problem:** accurate, accessible and comparable 3D reconstruction for real-world scenes and objects.
- **Role:** researcher and software developer.
- **Methods:** Structure from Motion, NeRF, Gaussian Splatting, point cloud evaluation.
- **Applications:** archaeology, heritage, forensic analysis, traffic accidents, infrastructure and education.
