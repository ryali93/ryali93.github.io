I am a geoinformatics researcher and geospatial developer working at the intersection of **geocomputing, GeoAI, Earth Observation, GIS software and applied innovation**.

My work focuses on building reproducible spatial data workflows, open-source geospatial tools and applied research systems for environmental monitoring, remote sensing, spatial data infrastructure and 3D geospatial reconstruction.

I combine a background in Geographic Engineering, a Master's degree in Computer Science and more than 10 years of experience across public institutions, universities and international research projects. My long-term goal is to help build a strong research group in Peru focused on geocomputing, GeoAI and open geospatial technologies.

## Research focus

- GeoAI and Earth Observation
- Geocomputing and spatial data engineering
- GIS software, WebGIS and spatial data infrastructure
- Environmental and risk monitoring
- 3D geospatial reconstruction
- Teaching, open science and research leadership

## Selected work

- **CloudSEN12** — global dataset for semantic understanding of clouds and shadows in Sentinel-2.
- **rgee** — R interface for Google Earth Engine and reproducible Earth Observation workflows.
- **RoadEye / Satellite AI monitoring** — AI-based infrastructure monitoring using super-resolution, semantic segmentation and WebGIS architecture.
- **SERNANP geospatial monitoring** — Earth Observation tools for protected areas, deforestation and LULC analysis.
- **3D Reconstruction Lab** — SfM, NeRF and Gaussian Splatting for applied 3D reconstruction.

## Collaboration

I am open to academic collaborations, applied research projects, GeoAI prototypes, open-source geospatial software, WebGIS systems and teaching initiatives.
